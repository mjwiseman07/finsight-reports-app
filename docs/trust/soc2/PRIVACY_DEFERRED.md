# Privacy TSC — Deferred to Phase 42.6 (Q2)

Per planning doc v1.10 Q2: SOC 2 Privacy TSC inclusion is deferred to Phase 42.6 / LOCK-42.6.1 for auditor decision. Founder position: HIPAA overlay (42.5V) is the dominant privacy program; SOC 2 Privacy TSC adds attestation overhead potentially redundant with HIPAA Compliance Pack at LOCK-42.6.7.

**Rationale:** SOC 2 Privacy TSC (P1.x through P8.x) and HIPAA Privacy Rule overlap on a substantial set of obligations (notice, choice, collection, use/retention, access, disclosure, quality, monitoring). For a single healthcare-overlay tenant class at LOCK-42.5.3 time, the marginal value of SOC 2 Privacy TSC ON TOP OF HIPAA Compliance Pack is unclear and is the auditor's call.

This document marks Privacy as **DEFERRED**, not OUT-OF-SCOPE. Auditor decision recorded against this placeholder at Phase 42.6A. If verticals other than healthcare are added in a future phase, SOC 2 Privacy TSC inclusion is reconsidered for that vertical.

**Decision gate:** LOCK-42.6.1 (engagement) → LOCK-42.6.3 (SOC 2 issuance) → also informs LOCK-42.6.7 (HIPAA Compliance Pack overlap)
