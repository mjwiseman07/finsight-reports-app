# Subcontractor BAA — Flow-Down Draft

**NOT FOR EXECUTION.** Founder-authored draft flow-down language for Wiseman Financial Technologies, LLC when engaging downstream subcontractors that may touch PHI. Counsel review required before execution.

## Purpose

When Advisacor engages a subcontractor that will create, receive, maintain, or transmit PHI on our behalf, a Business Associate Agreement with flow-down obligations per 45 CFR 164.308(b)(2) and 164.314(a) is required.

## Draft flow-down clauses (scaffolding only)

### 1. Subcontractor obligations

Subcontractor agrees to:

- Comply with the same restrictions and conditions on PHI that apply to Wiseman Financial Technologies, LLC under the upstream BAA
- Implement administrative, physical, and technical safeguards per 45 CFR 164.308–164.312
- Report security incidents and breaches per upstream BAA timing (founder position: 24-hour notification; HIPAA maximum 60 days to affected individuals)

### 2. Further subcontracting

Subcontractor shall not disclose PHI to further subcontractors without prior written authorization and executed downstream BAA.

### 3. Audit and inspection

Wiseman Financial Technologies, LLC retains audit rights consistent with upstream Covered Entity / BA agreements.

### 4. Termination

Upon termination, subcontractor shall return or destroy PHI per 45 CFR 164.504(e)(2)(ii)(J); destruction certified in writing.
