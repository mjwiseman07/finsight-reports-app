## Framework substitution rationale

FAR / CAS / DCAA are US-specific federal procurement regulations with no IFRS analog. IFRS reporters in the GovCon vertical use:

- **IFRS 8** operating segments + **IFRS 15.114** revenue disaggregation for contract-type concentration
- **IFRS 15.120–.122** expected satisfaction horizon for backlog (substituting funded vs unfunded terminology)

Documented in emitter headers, `framework_substitute_note` register field (C7b schema v1.2.0), and this decision doc.
